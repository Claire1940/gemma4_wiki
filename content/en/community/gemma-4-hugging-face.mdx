## FAQ

**Q: Can Gemma 4 run without an internet connection?**

A: Yes. Once you have downloaded the weights from **gemma 4 hugging face** or another source, the model runs entirely on your local hardware (CPU/GPU) without requiring a cloud connection.

**Q: What is the difference between the 26B and 31B models?**

A: The 26B model uses a Mixture of Experts (MoE) architecture, activating only about 3.8B parameters during inference for extreme speed and efficiency. The 31B model is a "dense" model, meaning it uses all parameters for every calculation, providing the highest possible quality and reasoning depth in the series.

**Q: Is Gemma 4 free for commercial use?**

A: Yes, Gemma 4 is released under the Apache 2.0 license, which allows for commercial use, modification, and distribution without a usage contract or cloud account.

**Q: How does Gemma 4 handle non-English languages?**
